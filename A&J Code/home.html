OUR MISSION

Ang & Jack's Eatery is a fine dining establishment that strives for inclusivity. ALL are welcome.

Our specialty is fusion cuisine. Ang & Jack fuse western, chinese, and Italian cooking to create some new and flavorful food.

This fusion of cuisines from all over the world, some family style dining, and inclusivity of the establishment help our fine dining establishment stand apart.

WHAT DOES FUSION COOKING MEAN TO US

Fusion cooking is a combination of different elements of culinary traditions that originate from different countries, regions, or cultures. It is literally a fusion of these different countries and cultures to create somthing new and interesting.

At Ang & Jack's Eatery this fusion creates an enviroment where all are welcome regardless of origins, backgrounds, or beliefs to come and enjoy a fine dining experience.
